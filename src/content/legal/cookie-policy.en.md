# Cookie policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

This policy describes how **Meshimo** (“we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on this website, and how you can control them. It should be read together with any privacy information we publish for this site.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, remember preferences, and understand how a site is used.

## How we use cookies

We group cookies into the categories below. You can accept or reject non-essential categories via the cookie banner or **Cookie settings** in the footer.

### Necessary

These cookies are required for the site to function and to keep it secure. They may include, for example, cookies that remember your cookie choices, maintain session state, or support infrastructure and security controls. Because they are essential, they cannot be turned off in our banner.

### Analytics

If you opt in, we use analytics tools to collect aggregated information about how visitors use the site (for example pages viewed and approximate location). On this site, measurement tools are loaded **only through Google Tag Manager** when you allow **analytics** storage, in line with Google’s **Consent Mode**. We do **not** embed the Google `gtag.js` snippet or Microsoft Clarity directly in our application code; those products can be configured inside GTM with the same consent requirements.

We do not use analytics cookies to identify you personally unless a tool you have allowed is configured to do so in GTM (we aim to use privacy-preserving settings where available).

## Third parties

Depending on your choices and our configuration in **Google Tag Manager**, data may be processed by providers such as:

- **Google** (e.g. Google Analytics 4) — privacy information: [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Microsoft** (e.g. Clarity), if enabled in GTM — privacy information: [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement)

These services receive only what their tags are configured to collect, subject to Consent Mode and your selections.

## Managing preferences

- Use **Cookie settings** in the site footer at any time to reopen the preference centre.
- You can also clear cookies for this site in your browser settings.

The consent manager we use is [Silktide Consent Manager](https://github.com/silktide/consent-manager) (open source, MIT). Choices are stored in your browser (for example under keys starting with `stcm.` or legacy `silktideCookieChoice_`).

## Contact

For questions about this policy or our use of cookies, contact us using the details on the **Contact** section of this website.
