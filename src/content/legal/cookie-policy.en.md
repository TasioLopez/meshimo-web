## 2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help a website
function properly, remember choices, measure performance, and understand how visitors use
the site.
We may also use similar technologies, such as scripts, tags, local storage, or pixels. In this
Cookie Policy, we refer to these together as “cookies” unless stated otherwise.

These cookies do not require consent, but we do inform you about them.
Examples may include:

## 4. How consent works on this website

When you first visit our website, you are shown a cookie banner. Through that banner, you can:
- accept all cookies;
- reject non-essential cookies;
- manage your cookie preferences.

Necessary cookies remain active because they are required for the website to operate properly.
Analytics cookies are only placed if you give consent.
You can withdraw or change your consent at any time through the cookie settings option
available on the website.


## 5. Cookies we use



### 5.1 Necessary cookies

These technologies are used to remember your cookie preferences and to support essential
website functions. On this website, the consent tool (Silktide Consent Manager, loaded from our
own domain) stores your choices in browser local storage (not as traditional HTTP cookies).
Under applicable law, this is often treated similarly to cookies when it is used to store
information on your device.
**Consent and preference storage (Silktide)**

The following local storage keys may be set on meshimo.com (first-party):

- **`stcm.hasConsented`** — Indicates that you have completed the initial cookie banner flow (accept, reject non-essential, or saved preferences).
- **`stcm.consent.necessary`** — Stores that necessary cookies/functionality are accepted (this category is required for the tool to operate).
- **`stcm.consent.analytics`** — Stores whether you have accepted or rejected analytics (true / false).
- **`stcm.consent.marketing`** — Stores whether you have accepted or rejected marketing (true / false).

**Legacy keys (migration only)**
If your browser still has data from an older Silktide key format, you may also see
silktideCookieChoice_necessary, silktideCookieChoice_analytics,
silktideCookieChoice_marketing, and/or silktideCookieBanner_InitialChoice. New visits typically
use the stcm.* keys above.

**Type:** first-party (local storage on your device for this website)

**Retention:** No fixed expiry in our implementation; data remain until you change or withdraw consent via Cookie settings, use any reset offered by the tool, or clear site data / local storage for this website (meshimo.com) in your browser.

**Other essential local storage (site functionality)**
We may also use local storage to remember your display preferences, such as a light/dark
mode preference, where applicable. This is separate from analytics and marketing and is used
only for basic site functionality.


### 5.2 Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect aggregated information about how visitors use the
website, such as which pages are visited, how users navigate through the site, and how the site
performs.
Google states that GA4 JavaScript tags use first-party cookies such as _ga and
ga<container-id> to distinguish users and persist session state, with a default expiration of 2
years unless configured otherwise.
Cookies that may be used include:

- **`_ga`** — Purpose: distinguishes users. Type: first-party. Retention: up to 2 years.
- **`ga<container-id>`** — Purpose: persists session state. Type: first-party. Retention: up to 2 years.

We only use Google Analytics after you have given consent for analytics cookies.


### 5.3 Analytics cookies: Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to better understand how visitors interact with our website through
tools such as heatmaps and session recordings. Microsoft explains that Clarity typically uses non-essential cookies and that, with consent mode, it waits for valid consent before setting cookies. Microsoft also lists cookies such as `_clck` and `_clsk` for Clarity’s normal operation.

Cookies that may be used include:

- **`_clck`** — Purpose: persists the Clarity user ID and preferences for the site. Type: first-party. Retention: may vary based on Clarity/browser settings.
- **`_clsk`** — Purpose: connects multiple page views by a user into a single Clarity session recording. Type: first-party. Retention: may vary based on Clarity/browser settings.
Depending on Microsoft’s implementation and your browser, Clarity may also involve additional
Microsoft-related cookies. Microsoft states that Clarity data retention differs by data type, with
recordings retained for 30 days and heatmaps/click data for up to 13 months.
We only use Microsoft Clarity after you have given consent for analytics cookies.
